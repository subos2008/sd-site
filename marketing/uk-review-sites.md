## What this means

- **Every viable target (1–6) is an SEO/content operation** — organic
  traffic, near-zero marginal cost per click, no ad spend to recycle.
  Exactly the profile that tolerates lifetime revshare (see
  [affiliate-program-terms.md](affiliate-program-terms.md)); the
  friction will be our unproven conversion, not the revshare structure.
- All of the top five already carry Secret Benefits and/or Seeking, so
  the category's affiliate economics are familiar to them.
- **Counterparty quality varies enormously**: two verifiable European
  companies with named humans (Webels ApS, Fichteberg GmbH) vs a cluster
  of anonymous US-addressed operations with persona bylines. Contracts
  and payout relationships favour the former; rankings favour reaching
  the latter too. Sequence accordingly.
